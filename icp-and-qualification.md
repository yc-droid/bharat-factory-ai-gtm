# ICP + Qualification — Bharat Factory AI

**Last updated:** 2026-04-21
**Owner:** SalesUp SDR team (tier TLs: Anjali, Jasmine, Miskat)
**Goal of pilot:** Close **700 workers** across paid pilots in 1 week.

---

## 1. The Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

A factory owner or plant head who decides on HR, IT, and operations for the plant, and whose plant fits **all 5** of the below.

| Dimension | Must-have (A-tier) | Nice-to-have (B-tier) | Disqualifier |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Worker count on-floor** | 100–2,000 blue-collar workers in a single location | 50–100 | <50 or >5,000 (<50 = low data yield; >5,000 = contract cycle too long for pilot) |
| **Operation type** | Manual / semi-manual assembly, stitching, packaging, QC, machining, material handling | Light automation mixed with manual lines | Fully automated / lights-out factories |
| **Industry** | Textile / garment, auto components, electronics assembly, FMCG packaging, metal fabrication, footwear, plastics | Pharma packaging, jewellery, food processing, logistics sorting | Food / chemical plants with strict hygiene/PPE rules that block head-cams |
| **Geo** | **Hubli, Bangalore, Chennai/Sriperumbudur, Coimbatore, Ahmedabad/Sanand, Nashik** | Pune, Hyderabad, Tirupur, Jaipur | Surat (worker shortage), Gurgaon/Manesar (union issues), NCR/Delhi (Q2 disruption), Kashmir/NE |
| **Worker tenure / IR climate** | Stable multi-year workforce, company-managed | Mix of own + contract workers | Unionized with active disputes, aggressive wage demands, strike history |

**A-tier = priority day-1 list.** B-tier = backlog for week 2. C/out = skip entirely.

## 2. Who we speak to (decision map)

**Primary decision maker (the close):**
- Owner / MD / CEO (family-run factories)
- Plant Head / Works Manager (pro-managed)

**Secondary (influencers, useful for warm intros):**
- HR Head — signs off on worker comms + consent
- IT Head — sometimes flagged for "data" concerns (easy dismiss: it's a head-cam, not a network tool)
- Production / Ops Head

**Gatekeeper drill (receptionist / EA):**
> "We are Bharat Factory AI. We work with factories to run a zero-cost AI efficiency study — like the ones Reliance and Raymond have done. Just need 3 minutes with the plant head to explain what's in it for the factory."

## 3. Qualification flow (on the call)

**Discovery — must get all 4 before pitching:**

1. **Size:** "How many workers do you have on the floor in this plant?"
   → If <50 → thank, close out politely, skip.
   → If 50–2,000 → continue.

2. **Operation type:** "Are these workers mostly on manual/assembly lines, or machine-tended?"
   → Manual / semi-manual → A-tier, full pitch.
   → Heavy automation → soft-pitch, deprioritize.

3. **Authority:** "Who makes the call on new HR/ops initiatives here — you or someone else?"
   → If not DM → get DM name + number, schedule direct. Don't pitch to non-DM.

4. **Timing:** "If this turned out to be a fit, could you start a 5–7 day data collection next week?"
   → Yes → close immediately.
   → "Need to think / discuss" → follow referral close (see below).
   → "Maybe next month" → nurture to Week 2 bucket.

| **D — Disqualify** | Fails ICP, <50 workers, disqualifying industry, wrong geo | Polite exit, no follow-up, remove from list. |

---

## 5. Disqualifiers (hard no — exit politely)

- <50 workers on one floor
- Chemical / pharma plants with strict PPE + cleanroom (head-cam won't fit)
- Active union disputes or strike history in last 12 months
- Factories in Surat, Gurgaon, Manesar, NCR general (Q2 election disruption)
- Factories with proprietary / IP-sensitive processes where video recording is disallowed (defense, pharma R&D, semiconductor)
- Factory owner says "we already work with Scale / Surge / Karya" — exit, competitor-locked

## 6. Compliance guardrails

- **Every factory must sign an NDA before data collection** (Build.ai template, already drafted)
- **Worker consent form** required before headset placement — even though they're paid, consent is mandatory
- **No mention of Build.ai on the cold call** — only "Bharat Factory AI"
- **Build.ai revealed only at demo / MOU stage**, under NDA
