Common questions

What factory owners ask us.

Is it really free? What's the catch?

Genuinely zero cost. No setup fee, no hardware fee, no commitment after the pilot. We fund everything because the data your workers generate trains the next generation of factory automation systems. That's where our returns come from — not from you.

What about data privacy and our trade secrets?

We sign a mutual NDA before any data collection. The headset records worker hand movements and general environment — not faces, not conversations, not proprietary machinery. You decide what's off-limits.

Will workers be comfortable wearing a headset?

Worker consent is mandatory. Most workers welcome it because a portion of the monthly payout goes directly to them (~₹1,400/worker/month in extra income). The headset is lightweight and safety-certified.

How long do we have to commit?

Nothing. The pilot is 5–7 days. After you see the efficiency report and receive the payout, you decide whether to continue monthly. Zero lock-in.

Why are you offering this for free?

Every month we delay, factories in other countries get onboarded by our competitors. India has the chance to become the global manufacturing AI leader — but only if Indian factories partner first. That's why we're investing.

When do the robots roll out? What's guaranteed?

Founding partners get first access when our humanoid and automation systems roll out. The guarantee is simple: the first 100 partner factories get locked-in pricing + priority deployment. Everything else is spelled out in the contract.
