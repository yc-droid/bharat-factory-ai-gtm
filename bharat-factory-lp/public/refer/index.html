Common questions

What everyone asks before their first intro.

When exactly do I get paid?

The day the factory pilot officially starts (NDA signed + hardware deployed). We transfer ₹25,000 via UPI or bank the same day, typically within 48 hours of pilot-live. Proof of transfer shared on WhatsApp.

What counts as a "closed" intro?

A factory where (1) we reach them via your intro, (2) they sign the NDA, and (3) the 5–7 day pilot actually starts. You get paid at step 3. If steps 1–2 happen but they pull out before hardware deployment, no payment.

Is there any cap on how many intros I can make?

No. Zero cap. 50 closed intros = ₹12.5 lakh in your account. We want volume. As long as each intro is a genuine factory owner with 50+ workers, you earn.

Do I need to pitch the factory myself?

No. Just share their name + phone. We pitch, demo, close. You're welcome to sit in on the call if you want (and if the owner's comfortable), but it's not required.

What if someone else refers the same factory?

First submission wins — timestamp-based. We never double-pay or double-count. If the factory was already in our CRM from ads or cold calls before your intro, we'll show you proof.

Is this a pyramid/MLM scheme?

No. You get paid directly by Bharat Factory AI (private initiative, aligned with Make in India). You don't recruit other referrers, there's no downline. One intro → one payment. Clean.

Will my relationship with the owner be affected?

No — the factory owner actually gets ₹4 lakh/month in cash plus a free ₹20 lakh efficiency report. They'll thank you for the intro. We never pressure, never spam, never misrepresent.

Can I scale this into a side business?

Yes, many of our top referrers do exactly that. CAs, industrial RE brokers, and consultants often close 5–15 intros per month. We provide a referrer dashboard after your 3rd intro so you can track everything in real-time.
