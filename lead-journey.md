## Tier Tagging Cheat-Sheet

| Tier | Criteria | Treatment |
|---|---|---|
| A | 100+ workers, owner/plant head on call, ready for pilot in 2 weeks | Demo <48 hrs, NDA <72 hrs |
| B | 50–100 workers OR interested but 30–60 day timeline | Nurture, weekly check-in, case-study share |
| C | <50 workers or curious only | Monthly newsletter, no SDR time |
| D | Trader, no factory, competitor, wrong ICP | Disqualify, no WhatsApp |

## Critical Handoffs

- **SDR → Field Ops** (Stage 5): Handoff doc in CRM with call notes, factory address, tier, owner language preference.
- **Field Ops → Build.ai Hardware** (Stage 7): POC contact, floor layout photo, power/wifi availability.
- **Build.ai → SalesUp CSM** (Stage 8): Data stream live confirmation.
- **CSM → AM** (Stage 10): Pilot outcome, NPS, referral candidates.
