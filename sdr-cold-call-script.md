## 3. Full Pitch — 3-Minute Structured Flow

### Step 1 — Mission framing + "zero cost, zero risk" (repeat 2x)
> "Sir pehle positioning samajh lijiye. Bharat Factory AI **India ki industrial AI initiative** hai — **Make in India mission ke aligned, PLI Scheme-ready, Atmanirbhar Bharat vision** ke saath. Hum government program nahi hain, lekin government ke manufacturing revolution ke saath poori tarah aligned hain.
>
> Iska matlab kya? **Zero cost, zero risk**. Aapko ek rupay bhi nahi dena. NDA sign karte hain, data privacy 100% lock. **1,300+ Indian factories already ja chuki hain is process se, aur 10+ publicly listed companies** bhi humare saath hain — unki legal aur compliance team ne due diligence ki hai. Matlab ya toh yeh genuine hai, ya yeh genuine hai."

*Wait for acknowledgement — "haan theek hai / samajh gaya."* If no ack, repeat.

### Step 2 — Cash FIRST (the hook that closes)
> "Pehla incentive — **seedha cash aapke factory ko**. Hum ₹25 dete hain har usable hour of data ke liye. Matlab:
>
> 1 worker × 5 usable hours/day × 30 days × ₹25 = **₹3,750 per worker/month**
> 100 workers = **₹4 lakh/month**, cash, aapko.
>
> Bas headset pehnana hai workers ko, 1 mahine ke liye. Aur kuch nahi."

**Payout table (read aloud if they ask "aur agar zyada workers hain?"):**

| Workers | Monthly payout |
|---|---|
| 100 | ₹4 lakh |
| 200 | ₹8 lakh |
| 500 | ₹20 lakh |

> "Sir, seedha baat — **1,000 workers ka factory hai toh ₹40 lakh ek mahine mein**. Zero investment."

### Step 3 — AI Efficiency Report (₹20 lakh value, free)
> "Doosra — **ek AI efficiency report jo market mein koi nahi deta**. Value hai around ₹20 lakh, aapko free. Isme milega:

> Private portal login milega. Jab chaahein dekh lein."

### Step 4 — Early access to factory robots
> "Teesra — hum jald hi automation systems aur humanoid robots factories mein deploy karne waale hain. Jo factories abhi humare saath partner hain, unko **founding partner pricing** milegi — subsidized rate, pehla access. Aapka competitor peeche reh jayega."

### Step 5 — Close line

## 4. Qualification Questions (ask all 4 before booking)

| # | Question | Use |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | "Sir, is plant mein on-floor workers kitne hain?" | Size — <50 disqualify, 100–2,000 ideal |
| 2 | "Workers mostly manual assembly pe hain ya machine-tended?" | Op-type — manual = A-tier |
| 3 | "HR aur ops ke naye initiatives ka decision aap lete hain ya koi aur?" | Authority — if not DM, get DM contact |
| 4 | "Agar fit baithta hai, next week 5–7 din ka data collection possible hai?" | Timing — yes = close now |

**Tier logic (assign at call end):**
- **A — Hot:** DM on call + 100+ workers + manual ops + hot geo (Hubli/BLR/Chennai/Coimbatore/Ahmedabad/Nashik) + verbal yes → WhatsApp deck + demo in 24 hrs.
- **B — Warm:** Fits ICP but needs 2nd convo → 48-hr follow-up scheduled.
- **C — Nurture:** Non-DM or "not now" → weekly drip.
- **D — Disqualify:** <50 workers / wrong industry / wrong geo / union issues → polite exit.

## 8. DO-NOT-SAY List (hard rules)

- **NEVER say "Build.ai"** — brand on call is only "Bharat Factory AI."
- **NEVER say "AI will replace workers"** — say "AI helps workers perform better."
- **NEVER quote in USD** — INR only, always.
- **NEVER claim we are a government scheme, program, body, or ministry initiative.** We are **aligned with** Make in India / PLI / Atmanirbhar Bharat — we are not sponsored by the government. Misrepresenting this is illegal under the IT Act.
- **NEVER use government seals, logos, emblems, or official letterheads** in any comm.
- **NEVER mention a specific minister, department, or ministry name** as a partner.
- Use words like: *aligned with*, *supporting*, *part of India's*, *initiative*, *mission*, *vision*.
- Avoid: *scheme*, *program* (as in sarkari), *sponsored*, *approved by*, *registered under*.
- **NEVER promise numbers beyond this script** — ₹4L/100 workers, ₹25/hour, ₹20L report value, ₹25k referral. Nothing else.
- **NEVER ask for money** — zero cost is the whole pitch.
- **NEVER commit to specific robot launch dates** — "jald hi" / "coming soon" only.
- **NEVER reveal competitor names** (Scale / Surge / Karya) unless they bring it up.
- **NEVER bypass NDA** — every data collection must have NDA signed first.
