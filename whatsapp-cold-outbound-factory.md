# Cold Outbound WhatsApp — Track A (Direct Factory Owners)

**Use case:** The SDR has the factory owner's number from the Blitz data pull but hasn't reached them on a live call yet. WhatsApp is first-touch.

**Brand:** Bharat Factory AI (never "Build.ai")
**Framing:** 🇮🇳 aligned with Make in India · PLI · Atmanirbhar Bharat
**Tone:** Respectful Hinglish · direct · dhanda-mindset · short

---

## Guardrails

- **Never** mention "Build.ai" — always "Bharat Factory AI"
- **Never** claim govt scheme / sponsorship — only *aligned with*
- **Never** make specific payout promises before qualification (use ranges: "₹4–8L")
- **Never** send audio recordings of Varun — use LP video links only
- **Always** include the LP URL for credibility
- **Always** sign with SDR name + brand
- Max 3 touches before moving lead to nurture bucket (D tier)
