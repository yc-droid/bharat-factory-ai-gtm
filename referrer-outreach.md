## Guardrails

- **Never** promise specific intro conversion rates
- **Never** claim govt-sponsored / govt-funded
- **Always** frame as "aligned with Make in India"
- **Always** route intros through the `/refer` form OR WhatsApp with structured fields
- **Always** pay within 48 hours of pilot start (trust-building)
- **Always** keep the referrer updated (intro received → NDA sent → pilot start → cash paid)
