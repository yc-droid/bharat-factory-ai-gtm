# Efficiency Report Walkthrough — Varun (3:28)
Source: `Build AI - Sample Efficiency Report.mp4`

Hello everyone, I'm Varun from Build and we're trying to bring the cost of labor down to zero by collecting data. We'd love to partner with your factory and in exchange we bring this **labor efficiency report at zero cost to you**. This report costs **$25,000 to make for every thousand workers**.

Every single factory owner is going to get one website all for themselves, where they can log in using their **unique ID/password exclusive to themselves**. And this is the report that we bring to you.

This report is a sample efficiency report from a factory recorded on 12th of March with **600 workers**. About this report: what we are doing, coverage across India, and the methodology.

**Methodology:**
- We put our devices on the heads of your workers for **5 to 7 days**
- We record on an average **7.7 hours per day at 30 frames per second**
- Also have a **6-axis IMU stream at 50 Hz**
- The annotated pipeline clearly tells if the worker is working or not

**What the report delivers:**

1. **Factory index vs 300+ factories** — "Build is the only company in the entire world who can offer this to you because we are the only one who has access to this data. We've partnered with some pretty big names — 6+ industries that have been publicly listed."

2. **Productivity curve by hour** — the strongest hour is 9–10am, weakest is 6–7pm. As the day goes, productivity drops.

3. **Worker productivity distribution** — a spike at 60%. Majority of workers at 60% efficiency. We can increase this using our improvement plan.

4. **Economic impact of productivity** — via optional survey. "This is what you pay your workers and this is how much you can change and how you can optimize."

5. **Per-worker analysis** (the most expensive part of the report). "If there are 1,000 workers, if there are 100 workers, if there are 20,000 workers — we give you a per-worker analysis for all the 20,000 workers." Every device has a unique ID mapped to the video recording.

6. **Improvement plan** — based on industrial parameters standard to every industry + the collected data. Priority interventions, implementation roadmap, projected outcomes.

7. **Individual worker page** — click a worker, get their entire day's view: when they're working, when they're not, when they're using their mobile phone.

**Again, all of this at completely zero cost.**
